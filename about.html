Our Story

Zuri Nilotica was born from a simple conviction: the most extraordinary skincare ingredients already exist in nature — they simply need to be treated with respect.

Africa's shea belt stretches across thousands of kilometres of savannah, where wild Vitellaria nilotica trees produce fruit unlike anything cultivated in a laboratory. For centuries, communities across East and West Africa have harvested, processed, and used shea butter as a daily essential.

We began by listening. To the women who harvest. To the land that provides. To the science that validates what tradition has always known. The result is Zuri Nilotica — a skincare brand that brings these ingredients to the world without compromising their origin, their purity, or their story.

Our Name

Zuri means "beautiful" in Swahili — not beauty as ornament, but beauty as truth, as something honest and unadorned.

Nilotica refers to Vitellaria nilotica, the East African shea tree — a botanical treasure that produces a butter prized for its exceptional softness, higher olein content, and delicate, naturally sweet fragrance. Together, the name speaks to what we believe: that real beauty begins at the source.
