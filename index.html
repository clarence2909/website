Our Origin

Born in Uganda. Carried by Tradition.

Deep in the lush landscapes of northern Uganda, where the Nile flows quietly through ancient savannah, grows something extraordinary — the Nilotica shea tree. For generations, communities here have harvested its seeds by hand, pressing them slowly and carefully into one of nature's most potent butters.

Zuri Nilotica exists to bring that heritage to you — pure, traceable, and never compromised.

Why Nilotica Shea?

Origin

Sourced from Uganda's Nile belt, where the Nilotica variety grows exclusively. Softer, silkier, and richer than West African shea — it's nature's rarest butter.

Purity

No additives. No bleaching. No refining. What leaves Uganda is exactly what arrives at your door — raw goodness, nothing more.

Impact

Every purchase supports the women harvesters and farming communities in northern Uganda who have tended these trees for centuries.

Versatility

From deep skin nourishment to hair moisture and scar healing — Nilotica shea does more with less. A little goes a long way.

Rooted in Responsibility

Zuri Nilotica is more than a skincare brand — it's a commitment. We work directly with Ugandan harvesters, pay fair wages, and champion sustainable wild-harvesting practices that protect the Nilotica tree for future generations. When you choose Zuri, you choose a product with a story worth telling.
